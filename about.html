About Us

Kalka Mata
Nagelia  family was a result of one of Maharaj Agrasen’s progeny named Mittal thus, we got our gotra as Mittal. Our ancestors settled in a small  village known as Nangolia, situated 70km away from another  village Kalukabas of Jaipur District. Due to outbreak of a  disease in Nangolia, our ancestors migrated to Kalukabas. Thus, our ancestors  were called “Nangolia” by the residents of Kalukabas. Later on Nangolia was  pronounced as Nagelia.

Here we would also like to mention that we Nagelia’s  can put any of the three surnames i.e. “Agarwal” as a progeny of Maharaj  Agrasen, “Mittal” significance of our gotra, and “Nagelia” as our  origin  village. We worship Kalka Mata as our Kul Devi.

Kalka Mata Temple
There is a big temple of Kalka Mata few kilometers away from the village Kalukabas. The temple is situated on the top of a mountain and only way to reach there is by foot climbing more than 500 steps. This is where we perform Jarula Ceremony (Mundan) of our children.

Our great  great grandfather Late Ramchandra ji was born in the village  Kalukabas. Ramchandra ji was blessed with three sons. The eldest son was  Late Bakshiram ji, second in the line was Late Badriprasad ji and the youngest son was Late Shivprasad ji. They were our great grandfather.

We form the  generation after our great grandfathers. Eldest son Bakshiram ji was blessed with four sons called Jainarayan ji, Mungilal ji, Sualal ji and Balabaksh ji. Jainarayan ji’s only son was Madanlal ji. Madanlal ji has spent  his entire life in kalukabas with his family. Badriprasad ji had no children of his own so, he adopted Nandlal ji, the eldest son of Shivprasad  ji. Nandlal ji’s son was Lakshminarayan ji. Youngest  Shivprasad ji was blessed with four sons namely Nandlal ji(adopted by  Badriprasad ji), Shivnarayan ji, Bansidhar ji and Nathulal ji. Shivnarayan ji, son of Shivprasad ji was the first person of  Nagelia family who migrated to Jamshedpur for better prospects in business in  the year 1928. He dreamt for a happy and prosperous Negelia family so, he called  all his brothers and parents to Jamshedpur. All his  brothers and parents came to Jamshedpur. Now his dreams have come true.

Nagelia family is one of the biggest families of Jamshedpur with more  than 150 members. Everyone is well established and known individual of  Jamshedpur. We “Nagelia family” pray to God for happiness, prosperity and growth  in everyone’s life. Details of each and every member of our family can be found  in the Family Tree section.
